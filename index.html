EHR-Vis



Electronic Health Records (EHR) are digital versions of a patient's medical history. Time series data within EHRs captures patient health measurements over time, aiding in monitoring, treatment evaluation, and prediction. Irregularly sampled time series in EHRs, with varying time intervals, can be challenging to analyze. Visualization plays a crucial role in understanding patterns and identifying gaps or anomalies in the data. Visual representations such as line graphs or scatter plots help healthcare professionals interpret the time series effectively. EHRs, along with visualization of time series data, enhance care coordination, decision-making, and personalized patient care.

t-SNE Visualization

t-SNE (t-Distributed Stochastic Neighbor Embedding) is a machine learning technique used for visualizing high-dimensional data by mapping it into a lower-dimensional space while preserving local relationships.
